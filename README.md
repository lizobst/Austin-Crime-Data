### Z-score Change in Crime by Year

<img width="496" alt="image" src="https://github.com/lizobst/Austin-Crime-Data/assets/167817524/0b01ee20-6657-4355-98d6-de388d126a5f">


Poisson Z-score used to determine significant increase or decrease in crime per year by APD district. Z-score calculated as 2 * [ Square_Root(Current) - Square_Root(Historical) ]. Significant increase in crime is signified by a Z-score >=3 and significant decrease in crime is signified by a Z-score <=-3. 

Crime has significantly been decreasing across all districts throughout the 4 years. The biggest decrease in crime occurred from 2020 to 2021. District S has had the largest significant increase in crime from 2020 to 2021. They also had the second largest significant increase from 2021 to 2022. District 4 had the largest significant decrease from 2020 to 2021.
